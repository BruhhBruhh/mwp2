Who is moreworldpeace...

Moreworldpeace is a high energy dj who commands the dance floor.

From his native west coast home to his new home in New York City.

His crowd perception is heightened by his experience as on-stage talent. Opening for Kendrick Lamar [Pomo, Sango, & others] his experience on the mic and in the booth can turn any party into a signature experience.

His music selection is an extension of his varied work as an artist and producer. His deep love of contemporary hip hop, dance music, and unheard gems is reflected in each set. Djing since 2014, his taste maker arrangement brings electricity.
